About Luxxo Homes

More than a real estate
agency.

We don't just sell homes. We create them.
Luxxo Homes is part of Casa Marbella Group — together with Casa Renovo Marbella, our own renovation company on the Costa del Sol.

We buy properties, renovate them to an exceptional standard, and bring them to market through Luxxo Homes. We also guide buyers in finding other properties — but only those that genuinely meet our standard.

How it started

From the Netherlands
to Marbella.

It started with an insight that set everything in motion. From our own company in the Netherlands — specialising in kitchens, bathrooms and flooring — we saw more and more people dreaming of life on the Costa del Sol. The climate, the calm, the lifestyle. But we also saw how hard it was to turn that dream into reality.

Because anyone buying a property in Marbella wants just one thing: to enjoy it. Not to wait endlessly, not to doubt the quality of the work, not to hope it finishes on time. Managing a renovation from abroad — from the Netherlands, Belgium or anywhere else — is a challenge you shouldn't underestimate. The reality is that working with local contractors in Spain often turns out differently than expected. Yet on the most beautiful locations there are stunning properties that, with the right hands, deserve a completely new life.

The bridge was quickly made. By working with the best materials — sourced directly from our own supply channels in the Netherlands — and our own construction team that works to Western European standards, we could close that gap. That's how Casa Renovo Marbella was born — and the Spanish dream became attainable without compromise.

What we do

Move-in ready.
Down to the last detail.

At Casa Renovo Marbella, it's about much more than renovation. We completely redesign spaces — more practical, more modern, lighter. Then comes interior design and styling at a high-end level. The result is a home where everything is right: from the layout to the lighting, from the materials to the last cushion on the sofa.

For the buyer, that means one thing: walk in, close the door behind you, and start enjoying immediately. No hassle, no unexpected costs. Just the life you dreamed of.
