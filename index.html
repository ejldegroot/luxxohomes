Aankoopbegeleiding

Buying property in Spain — guided by experts

Purchasing a home in Spain as a foreign buyer involves more steps than you might expect. We take care of every detail — so your path from search to keys is clear, safe, and enjoyable.

We know the Spanish market from the inside — not as outsiders, but as people who build, invest and live here ourselves. That perspective makes all the difference.

01

Personal intake & wishlist

We start with an in-depth conversation about your lifestyle, priorities, and budget. We then curate a selection of properties that truly match your criteria — no wasted viewings, no generic lists.

02

Viewings & area guidance

We personally accompany you on viewings and give you honest, independent advice on each property and location. We know every neighbourhood — the advantages and the pitfalls.

03

Legal & financial due diligence

We connect you with trusted independent lawyers and advisors who check the legal status of the property, arrange your NIE number, and structure the transaction correctly. No surprises.

04

Negotiation & offer

We negotiate on your behalf — with market knowledge, professionalism, and the right timing. Our goal is to get you the best possible price and terms.

05

Signing & completion

We guide you through the notary process, ensure all documents are in order, and are present at completion. We handle the handover so everything goes smoothly.

06

After-sale support

Our relationship doesn't end at the notary. We can assist with utilities, community registration, renovation introductions, and anything else you need to settle in comfortably.

The bridge between the Netherlands and Spain

We know the pitfalls.
Because we've been there.

Buying in Spain sounds attractive. And it is — if you know what you're doing. The market works differently, the regulations are different, and local contractors don't always deliver what they promise. We know these pitfalls inside and out.

Our greatest advantage: we operate on both sides of the border. Jacco runs a company in the Netherlands from which we supply kitchens, bathrooms and flooring for every renovation project. Not because it's easy — but because it means we keep full control over quality. No surprises on site. No compromises on materials.

We advise clients in the Netherlands who are considering a purchase, and guide them all the way through to the keys in their hand in Spain. One team, from start to finish.
